## General Settings

Navigate to **Settings** to configure your organization.

### Organization Details

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Name | Your organization's display name |
| Country | Primary country of operation |
| Base Currency | Default currency for reports |
| Financial Year Start | First month of your financial year |
| Tax ID | Your business tax identification number |

## Billing

Navigate to **Settings > Billing** to manage your subscription, view invoices, and update payment methods.

## Team Members

Navigate to **Settings > Members** to manage who has access to your organization.

### Inviting Members

1. Click **Invite Member**
2. Enter the email address
3. Select a role
4. The invited user receives an email to join

### Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| Owner | Full access, can delete the organization |
| Admin | Full access except organization deletion |
| Member | Standard access to all modules |
| Viewer | Read-only access |

## Advisors

Navigate to **Settings > Advisors** to invite external advisors (accountants, bookkeepers) with controlled access to your books.
