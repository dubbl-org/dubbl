dubbl includes 10+ financial reports organized by category.

## Core Reports

### Trial Balance
Summary of all account balances verifying that total debits equal total credits. Use this to check your books before generating other reports.

### Balance Sheet
Assets, liabilities, and equity at a specific point in time. Follows the accounting equation: Assets = Liabilities + Equity.

### Income Statement
Revenue and expenses over a period. Shows gross profit, operating income, and net income.

### Profit & Loss
Detailed P&L with comparison periods and date range filtering.

## Receivables & Payables

### Aged Receivables
Outstanding customer invoices grouped by aging buckets:
- Current (not yet due)
- 1-30 days overdue
- 31-60 days overdue
- 61-90 days overdue
- 90+ days overdue

### Aged Payables
Same aging analysis for outstanding supplier bills.

## Accounting Reports

### General Ledger
Complete transaction log for all accounts with running balances. Filter by date range.

### Cash Flow Statement
Cash inflows and outflows categorized as:
- **Operating** — Day-to-day business
- **Investing** — Asset purchases/sales
- **Financing** — Loans, equity

### Budget vs Actual
Compare budgeted amounts against actual spending. See [Budgets](/docs/modules/budgets) for details.

## Date Range Filtering

Most reports support custom date ranges with presets:
- This Month / Last Month
- This Quarter
- This Year / Last Year
- Custom range
