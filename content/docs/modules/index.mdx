title: Modules
description: Overview of all dubbl modules and features.
---

dubbl is organized into modules that cover the full accounting lifecycle.

## Core Accounting

- [Chart of Accounts](/docs/modules/chart-of-accounts) — Manage your general ledger accounts
- [Journal Entries](/docs/modules/journal-entries) — Record double-entry transactions

## Sales

- [Contacts](/docs/modules/contacts) — Manage customers and suppliers
- [Invoicing](/docs/modules/invoicing) — Create, send, and track invoices
- [Quotes](/docs/modules/quotes) — Send quotes and convert to invoices

## Purchases

- [Bills](/docs/modules/bills) — Track supplier bills and payments
- [Purchase Orders](/docs/modules/purchase-orders) — Create and manage purchase orders
- [Expense Claims](/docs/modules/expenses) — Submit and approve expense claims

## Banking

- [Bank Accounts](/docs/modules/banking) — Manage bank accounts and transactions
- [Reconciliation](/docs/modules/banking) — Match bank transactions to records

## Advanced

- [Projects & Time Tracking](/docs/modules/projects) — Track billable hours
- [Inventory](/docs/modules/inventory) — Manage stock levels
- [Fixed Assets](/docs/modules/fixed-assets) — Track depreciation
- [Payroll](/docs/modules/payroll) — Process employee payroll
- [Budgets](/docs/modules/budgets) — Create and track budgets

## Reports

- [Financial Reports](/docs/modules/reports) — Trial balance, P&L, balance sheet, cash flow, and more

## Settings

- [Tax Rates](/docs/modules/tax-rates) — Configure tax rates
- [Currencies](/docs/modules/currencies) — Multi-currency support
- [Audit Log](/docs/modules/audit-log) — Track all changes
