The Documents module provides centralized file storage for your organization. Navigate to **Documents** in the sidebar.

## Features

- Upload and store business documents (contracts, receipts, statements)
- Organize files by type or category
- Attach documents to transactions, invoices, bills, and expenses
- Search and filter your document library

## Uploading Documents

Click **Upload** on the documents page to add files. Supported formats include PDF, images (PNG, JPG), and common office formats.

## Document Attachments

Documents can be attached to:

- **Invoices and quotes** - Attach supporting documents for customers
- **Bills and expenses** - Store receipts and supplier documents
- **Journal entries** - Attach source documents for audit purposes

## Storage

Documents are stored in S3-compatible storage. See [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started) for S3 configuration.
