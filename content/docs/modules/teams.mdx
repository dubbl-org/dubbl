The Teams module helps you organize your workforce and manage team structures. Navigate to **Teams** in the sidebar.

## Team Management

- Create teams and assign members
- Set team leads and roles
- View team workload and assignments

## Team Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| Member List | View all team members and their roles |
| Assignments | See what projects and tasks each member is working on |
| Capacity | Track team availability and workload |

## Integration with Other Modules

Teams integrate with:

- **Projects** - Assign team members to projects and tasks
- **Payroll** - Team structure informs payroll processing
- **Approval Workflows** - Route approvals based on team hierarchy

## Organization Members

For managing who has access to your dubbl organization (inviting users, setting permissions), see [Organization Settings](/docs/settings/organization).
