The Inventory module provides comprehensive stock management. Navigate to **Inventory** in the sidebar.

## Inventory Items

Track physical products with purchase prices, sale prices, and stock levels.

### Item Fields

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Code | Unique product code |
| Name | Product name |
| SKU | Stock keeping unit |
| Purchase Price | Cost to buy (cents) |
| Sale Price | Price to sell (cents) |
| Quantity on Hand | Current stock level |
| Reorder Point | Low stock threshold |

### Item Details

Each item has detail pages for:

- **Variants** - Size, color, or other variations of the same product
- **Suppliers** - Which suppliers provide this item and at what price
- **History** - Transaction history showing all stock movements

## Categories

Organize items into categories for easier browsing and reporting. Navigate to **Inventory > Categories**.

## Stock Adjustments

Adjust quantities from the item detail page:

- Enter a **positive number** to add stock
- Enter a **negative number** to remove stock
- Provide a **reason** for the adjustment

The system prevents negative stock levels.

## Low Stock Alerts

Items at or below their reorder point are highlighted with a **Low Stock** badge. Navigate to **Inventory > Alerts** for a consolidated view of all low-stock items.

## Warehouses

Track stock across multiple locations. See [Warehouses](/docs/modules/warehouses) for details.

## Stock Transfers

Move inventory between warehouses. Navigate to **Inventory > Transfers** to create and manage transfers.

## Stock Takes

Conduct physical inventory counts. See [Stock Takes](/docs/modules/stock-takes) for details.

## Assembly and Bill of Materials

Define how finished products are assembled from components. See [Assembly and BOM](/docs/modules/assembly-bom) for details.

## Inventory Valuation

Navigate to **Inventory > Valuation** to view the total value of your inventory, broken down by item and optionally by warehouse.

## Dashboard Stats

The inventory page shows:
- **Total Items** - Number of active products
- **Total Value** - Sum of (quantity x purchase price)
- **Low Stock** - Count of items at/below reorder point
