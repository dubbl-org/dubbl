## Tax Rates

Tax rates are configured per organization and can be applied to invoices, bills, and expenses. See [Tax Rates](/docs/modules/tax-rates) for setup details.

## Navigation

Access tax features from **Tax** in the sidebar. The tax dashboard shows an overview of your current tax obligations and upcoming deadlines.
