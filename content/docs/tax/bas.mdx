title: Business Activity Statement (BAS)
description: Generate Australian Business Activity Statements.
---

The BAS is a form submitted to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) to report GST, PAYG withholding, and other tax obligations. Navigate to **Tax > BAS** to generate your statement.

> **Disclaimer:** Australian tax rules change frequently. Always verify your BAS with a qualified tax professional or BAS agent before lodging. If you notice outdated information, please [open an issue](https://github.com/dubbl-org/dubbl/issues).

## Prerequisites

- Set up GST tax rates (typically 10%) in your organization settings
- Apply GST to invoices and expenses as appropriate
- Close the relevant tax period before generating

## BAS Sections

dubbl calculates the following sections:

### GST
- **1A** - GST on sales
- **1B** - GST on purchases
- **Net GST** - Amount to pay or refund

### PAYG Withholding
If you use the payroll module, PAYG amounts withheld from employee pay are included.

### PAYG Instalments
Estimated income tax instalments based on your reported income.

## Lodging

dubbl generates the data for your BAS. Lodge through the ATO Business Portal or your registered tax agent.

## Frequency

- **Monthly** - For businesses with GST turnover of $20 million or more
- **Quarterly** - Most common for small to medium businesses
