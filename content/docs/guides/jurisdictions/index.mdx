## Decision Matrix

### Lowest Effective Tax Rate
- **UAE Free Zones** - 0% corporate tax on qualifying income (9% above AED 375,000 from 2024)
- **US LLC** - 0% at entity level for non-US tax residents (pass-through)
- **Estonia** - 0% on retained earnings, 20% only when distributed
- **Hong Kong** - territorial taxation, offshore income exempt

### Easiest Setup
- **US Wyoming LLC** - fully remote, minimal requirements, no state income tax
- **UK Ltd** - same-day incorporation online via Companies House
- **Estonia e-Residency** - digital setup from anywhere, EU bank access

### Best Banking and Reputation
- **Singapore** - world-class banking, strong IP protection, AAA credit rating
- **Ireland** - EU member, English-speaking, strong fintech ecosystem
- **Netherlands** - EU core, extensive treaty network, reliable banking

### Digital Nomad Friendly
- **Estonia e-Residency** - designed for location-independent entrepreneurs
- **Portugal** - NHR successor regime, quality of life, EU residency path
- **UAE** - no personal income tax, modern infrastructure

## Common Combinations

- **US LLC + Portugal residency** - US LLC pass-through income, Portuguese NHR successor for low personal tax
- **Estonia OÜ + EU banking** - EU company with digital management, retain earnings tax-free
- **UK Ltd + UAE residency** - UK credibility with UAE personal tax benefits
- **Singapore + Hong Kong** - Asia-Pacific dual presence for China trade and ASEAN access
- **Ireland + Netherlands** - EU holding structure with strong treaty networks
