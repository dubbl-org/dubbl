Welcome to the **dubbl** documentation. dubbl is a full-featured, open-source alternative to Xero and QuickBooks built with Next.js, Drizzle ORM, and PostgreSQL.

## Features

- **Double-entry bookkeeping** with chart of accounts and journal entries
- **Invoicing & quotes** with PDF generation and payment tracking
- **Bills & purchase orders** for accounts payable
- **Banking & reconciliation** with CSV import
- **Expense claims** with approval workflows
- **Projects & time tracking** with billable hours
- **Inventory management** with stock tracking
- **Fixed asset management** with depreciation
- **Payroll** with tax calculations
- **Budgets** with variance analysis
- **10+ financial reports** including P&L, balance sheet, cash flow
- **Audit trail** for compliance
- **Multi-currency** support
- **API-first** with full REST API
